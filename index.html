Sandeep Jacob & how HOPE began

The company was founded in 2023 by Sandeep Jacob, who brought it forward as HOPE CREDIT FINANCE. He is the driving mind behind the decision to bridge the gap between people and financial institutions—with innovative ideas and day-to-day work rooted in moral principles and values.

What we do today

We began with personal loans across PAN India and have continued to grow—serving society through accessible credit. HOPE CREDIT FINANCE is committed to the law and to ethical standards. Customers need not worry about heavy documentation or formalities: our team takes care of the process end to end. Today we are among the largest DSAs in PAN India, with more than 400 team members working under one umbrella.

Frequently Asked
Questions?

Find answers to the most common questions about our loan products and application process.

You can apply for a loan directly through our website by filling out the online application form. The process takes less than 10 minutes and you'll receive a decision within 24 hours.

Typically you'll need: valid government ID, proof of income (pay stubs or bank statements), proof of address, and employment verification. Additional documents may be required depending on the loan type.

Most loan applications are reviewed and approved within 24 hours. Once approved, funds are typically disbursed to your bank account within 1–3 business days.

Our interest rates are competitive and depend on the lender, loan type, amount, tenure, and your profile. Use our loan calculator for a rough EMI illustration only—final pricing is always confirmed by the financing institution.
