Personal Loan

Get funds for weddings, medical emergencies, travel, or any personal need. Our personal loans come with competitive rates and structured documentation support—we connect you with banks and NBFCs and help you understand eligibility and next steps clearly.

Personal loans are unsecured: no collateral required. Tenures typically range from 6 to 60 months. Final approval, amount, and rate always depend on the lender's assessment of your profile and policy.

Questions

Personal Loan FAQs

Find answers to common questions about personal loans and how we support your application.

Typically identity and address proof (Aadhaar, PAN), income proof (salary slips, bank statements, or ITR), and employment or business details. The exact list varies by lender—we will share a checklist for your case.

Timelines depend on the lender and completeness of documents. Many applications move quickly once KYC and income verification are in order. We keep you updated at each stage.

Most lenders allow part-prepayment or foreclosure subject to their terms and any applicable charges. We explain these before you sign.

Formal loan applications usually involve a lender credit inquiry. We recommend sharing accurate information early so we can align you with suitable products and avoid unnecessary multiple applications.
