Home Loan

Whether you're buying your first home, upgrading to a bigger space, or building your dream house, our home loans offer competitive interest rates and flexible repayment options tailored to your needs. We understand that purchasing a property is one of the most significant financial decisions you'll make, and our team is here to guide you every step of the way.

From document verification to quick disbursal, we streamline the entire process so you can focus on what matters most—settling into your new home. With tenures of up to 30 years and loan amounts that cover a wide range of property values, we make homeownership accessible and affordable.

Home Loan FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our home loan products and application process.

You'll need identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN), income proof (salary slips, bank statements), property documents, and employment verification. We'll guide you through the complete checklist.

Home loans can be availed for up to 30 years, depending on your age and repayment capacity. Longer tenures mean lower EMI but higher total interest payable.

Yes, we offer home loans for under-construction properties. Disbursement is typically made in stages as per the construction progress, as per the builder agreement.

We offer both fixed and floating rate options. Floating rates are linked to benchmark rates and may change. Fixed rates remain constant throughout the loan term. Our team can help you choose the right option.
